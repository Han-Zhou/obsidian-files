
### Definition

Let R be a ring. 
[[Rings]]

We say an element a $\in$ R  is a <u>unit</u> 
if $\exists$ b $\in$ R s.t. $$ b \cdot a = a \cdot b = 1$$
we say an element e of R is an <u>idempotent</u> if $$e^2=e$$
