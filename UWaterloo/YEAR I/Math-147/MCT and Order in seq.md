### Monotone Convergence Thm 

Let $\{a_n\}$ be a sequence in $\mathbb{R}$ that is <u>monotone</u> (either decreasing or increasing)
$$\{a_n\} \ converges \ \iff \{a_n\} \ is \ bounded$$
